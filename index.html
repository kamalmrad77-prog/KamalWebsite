The Journey

Driven by Determination

I began my motorsport journey at just 6 years old on local Australian karting circuits. What started as a hobby quickly turned into a string of podium finishes and national level results. At fourteen, I made the bold decision to move overseas on my own to compete in international karting championships, gaining invaluable experience against the world's best young drivers.

By 15, I had stepped up to Formula 4 a major milestone in open-wheel racing before transitioning into GT machinery. Today, at seventeen, I race professionally in the Porsche Michelin Sprint Challenge as one of the youngest drivers in the field, using every event to refine my craft and showcase my distinctive driving style.

My journey reflects resilience and ambition, with an unconventional rise through the ranks. Each lap strengthens my position in professional motorsport and moves me closer to achieving success at the highest levels of racing.
